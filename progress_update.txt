I've implemented the **Settings Persistence** and **Keyboard Shortcuts**.

**Changes:**
1.  **Settings**: The player now remembers your **volume** and the **last video** you tuned into. It restores them automatically when you reload the page.
2.  **Controls**: Added keyboard support:
    -   `Space` / `k`: Play/Pause
    -   `Arrow Up/Down`: Volume +/-
    -   `Arrow Left/Right`: Seek +/- 5s
    -   `m`: Mute toggle
